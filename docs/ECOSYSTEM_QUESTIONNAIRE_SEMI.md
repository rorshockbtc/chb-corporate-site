# CHB Ecosystem Questionnaire - Semi

## Part 1: Product Identity

### One-liner (10 words or less)
Semi: Your AI that learns you, routes intelligently, costs less.

### Core Problem
AI assistants are expensive, forgetful, and one-size-fits-all. Every conversation starts over. Every provider charges the same regardless of query complexity. Semi solves this with intelligent routing - simple queries stay local (cheap), complex ones go external (when needed).

### "Aha Moment"
When you realize Semi answered a complex question without hitting the expensive external API - and the answer was just as good. "Wait, that didn't cost me extra credits?"

### Differentiator
- **Intelligent routing**: Uses the cheapest model that can handle your query
- **Learns continuously**: Gets smarter about YOU every day
- **Memory-aware**: Connected to Hash, knows your context
- **Transparent pricing**: See exactly what each interaction costs

## Part 2: User Personas

### Startup Founders (Blue)

**Biggest win**: "My AI costs dropped 60% and the quality actually improved because Semi learned our domain."

**Resonant language**: "Smart routing", "cost optimization", "AI that scales"

### Content Creators (Purple/Magenta)

**Biggest win**: "Semi writes in my voice now. First drafts sound like ME, not generic AI."

**Resonant language**: "Voice matching", "creative partner", "consistent tone"

### Memory Capturers (Green)

**Biggest win**: "Semi explains things at my pace. It remembers I like step-by-step instructions."

**Resonant language**: "Patient assistant", "remembers preferences", "no judgment"

## Part 3: Credits & Pricing

### Credit Consumption
Semi interactions DO cost credits (servers cost money to run). The cost is minimal but real:
- Simple queries (handled locally): Low credit cost
- Complex queries (external AI): Premium credit cost
- Learning/training: Included in background

### Usage Philosophy
- Credits should be consumed - this drives sustainable business
- Average users should exhaust monthly credits → incentive to upgrade
- Keep messaging general: "Very efficient, still settling on exact amounts"
- All apps use Semi as default router

### Value Articulation
"Semi is very efficient - we're still settling on exact credit amounts, but you'll get ample use from Comma and Period tiers. She routes intelligently, saving expensive API calls for when they're truly needed."

## Part 4: Ecosystem Integration

### Cross-Product Synergy
**Semi as the AI layer for everything**:
- **Hash → Semi**: Your memories inform Semi's responses
- **Pipes → Semi**: Semi synthesizes curator knowledge into answers
- **Semi everywhere**: Default AI across Hash, Pipes, Corporate site

### Data Flow
- Receives personality from Hash
- Queries Pipes for domain expertise
- Routes to external AI only when necessary

### Single Account Benefits
Semi knows you through Hash. One account means your AI assistant understands your communication style, past decisions, and preferences from day one.
