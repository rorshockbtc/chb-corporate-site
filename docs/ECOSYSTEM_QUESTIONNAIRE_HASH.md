# CHB Ecosystem Questionnaire - Hash

## Part 1: Product Identity

### One-liner (10 words or less)
Hash: Your private AI memory journal that never forgets.

### Core Problem
People repeat themselves constantly - to doctors, therapists, colleagues, AI assistants. Every conversation starts from scratch. Hash solves the "Don't Repeat Yourself" problem by giving you a persistent memory layer that travels with you.

### "Aha Moment"
When Semi references something you wrote in Hash three weeks ago without being asked - and it's exactly relevant to what you're working on now. "Wait, how did you know that?"

### Differentiator
- **Private by default**: Your journal stays yours unless you explicitly share
- **AI-native**: Written for AI to understand, not just humans to read  
- **Personality extraction**: Hash learns your voice, preferences, patterns
- **Cross-product memory**: Powers personalization across the CHB ecosystem

## Part 2: User Personas

### Startup Founders (Blue)
**Why they use Hash**: To maintain strategic continuity. Document decisions, pivot rationales, investor conversations - and have Semi recall them during critical moments.

**Biggest win**: "I was drafting a pitch and Semi reminded me of a framework I journaled about 6 months ago. Saved hours of reinvention."

**Resonant language**: "Strategic memory", "decision log", "founder's second brain"

### Content Creators (Purple/Magenta)  
**Why they use Hash**: To capture creative sparks and never lose ideas. The raw material that becomes content lives here first.

**Biggest win**: "I had a random thought in the shower, journaled it, and Semi helped me turn it into my most viral thread."

**Resonant language**: "Idea capture", "creative archive", "voice development"

### Memory Capturers (Green)
**Why they use Hash**: It IS their primary use case. The act of preserving memories, thoughts, family stories - Hash is built for them.

**Biggest win**: "I've been journaling about my grandmother's recipes. Now my daughter can ask Semi and get grandma's cooking wisdom in my voice."

**Resonant language**: "Legacy preservation", "family memory", "personal archive"

## Part 3: Credits & Pricing

### Credit Consumption
- Journal entries: Minimal cost (mostly storage)
- Memory retrieval by Semi: 1-3 credits depending on complexity
- Personality analysis: 5-10 credits
- Voice/pattern extraction: 10-20 credits

### Value Articulation
"Your memories are priceless. The credits you spend are for Semi to understand and recall them at exactly the right moment. You're not paying for storage - you're paying for intelligence."

## Part 4: Ecosystem Integration

### Cross-Product Synergy
**Hash → Semi**: Semi knows who you are because Hash told her. Your preferences, communication style, past decisions - all inform how Semi responds.

**Hash → Pipes**: Your expertise emerges from what you journal. Hash reveals what you know deeply, which can become curated Pipes.

### Data Sharing
- Personality insights → Semi (how to talk to you)
- Topic expertise → Pipes (what you know deeply)  
- Cognitive patterns → All products (how you think)

### Single Account Benefits
Your Hash is the foundation of your digital identity across CHB. One account means your entire history - every thought, every preference, every memory - travels with you and informs every interaction.
